Education standards and classroom excellence

Explore four professional education areas that support digital transformation, teaching quality, teacher competency, and international learning impact.

Microsoft Education

Microsoft Education provides professional teaching and digital learning support for modern classrooms by helping educators combine strong pedagogy with powerful technology tools.

ISTE Education

ISTE Education promotes standards-based digital teaching practice for educators and facilitators who want to use technology in ways that truly improve learning.

UNESCO ICT CFT

UNESCO ICT CFT, which stands for UNESCO Information and Communication Technology Competency Framework for Teachers, supports teacher competency development through structured ICT integration.

TRCN Nigeria

TRCN, the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria, supports teacher professionalism, registration, certification, and licensing while promoting strong teaching standards and professional growth across the education sector.

PISA Education

PISA Education represents international classroom quality, assessment readiness, and learner impact by focusing on how well students can apply knowledge in practical and real-life situations.
